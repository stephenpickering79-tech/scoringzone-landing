FAQ

Common Questions

How much can I earn?

It depends on how many students you refer. For example: 20 students × $9.99/month × 40% commission = $79.92/month in recurring income. The more you refer, the higher your tier.

Is there any cost to join?

No. The partner program is completely free to join. There are no fees, no minimums, and no obligations.

Do my students pay anything now?

No. Scoring Zone is currently in free early access — no credit card required. Your students can start using it today at no cost.

How do I track my referrals?

You’ll get access to a personal partner dashboard where you can see how many students signed up through your link, their activity, and your earnings.

When do commissions start?

Commissions begin when the app moves to paid subscriptions. By joining now and referring students during free early access, you lock in those referrals — your link stays attached to every student who signs up through it.

Can I try the app first?

Absolutely. Download Scoring Zone for free at scoringzone.app and try every feature yourself before recommending it to students.

What if a student I referred cancels?

You earn commission on active subscriptions only. If a student cancels, the commission stops — but your referral link stays attached. If they re-subscribe later, you earn again.
