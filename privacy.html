Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

Who We Are

Scoring Zone is a golf short game practice app built to help golfers improve through structured, scored drills. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website (www.scoringzone.net) and app.

Information We Collect

Account information: When you sign up, we collect your email address. This is used to create your account and send occasional updates about the app.

Practice data: Drill scores, round stats, XP, and progress data are stored to power your tracking, analytics, and Short Game Handicap. This data belongs to you — it’s what makes the app useful.

Usage data: We use Google Analytics 4 to understand how visitors use the website — which pages are visited, how long sessions last, and where traffic comes from. This data is anonymised and aggregated.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve the Scoring Zone app
  • Track your practice progress, stats, and Short Game Handicap
  • Send occasional product updates (you can unsubscribe at any time)
  • Understand how the website is used so we can make it better

We never sell your data to third parties.

Cookies

The website uses cookies from Google Analytics to understand site usage. These are analytical cookies — they don’t track you personally or serve ads. You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

We don’t use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or any third-party marketing tools.

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using cloud infrastructure with industry-standard encryption. We use Supabase for database services, which provides row-level security and encrypted connections. Access to your data is restricted to essential operations only.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics — website usage analytics
  • Vercel — website hosting
  • Supabase — database and authentication

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct any inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your practice data

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@scoringzone.net.

Data Retention

We keep your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all personal data and practice history is permanently removed within 30 days. You can request deletion at any time by emailing us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we’ll update the “last updated” date at the top of this page. We encourage you to check back periodically.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or how your data is handled, get in touch:

hello@scoringzone.net
